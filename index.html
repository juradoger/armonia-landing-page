What Does It Mean to Be an Armonía Amigo?

Amigos of Armonía are more than donors; they are conservation partners.

Your support directly protects Bolivia's birds and the extraordinary landscapes they depend on, from the Amazon rainforest to the Beni savannas, and the Andean valleys.

With your monthly contribution of $25, or even more, you make real, measurable conservation possible across Bolivia's most threatened ecosystems:
Your Impact

With Your Contribution, You Make Possible

Restore Native Habitats

Reforest degraded areas with native trees and palms, rebuild forest corridors, and recover critical feeding grounds.

critical feeding grounds for macaws, manakins, tanagers, and hundreds of other species

Safeguard Endangered Birds

Support conservation for Bolivia’s most threatened species, from the Blue-throated and Red-fronted

Macaws to the Palkachupa Cotinga, Horned Curassow and Royal Cinclodes.

Protect Key Reserves

Help maintain and defend more than 14,715 hectares of Armonía’s managed reserves

that shelter 350+ bird species and over 40 mammals.

Prevent & Control Wildfires

Create firefighting brigades, alliances, develop practical fire prevention strategies like firebreak

systems & controlled burning to protect the most important ecosystems of Bolivia.

Empower Local Communities

Promote wildlife-friendly livelihoods such as ecotourism, honey and fruit production, and

sustainable cattle ranching that create incentives for conservation.

Advance Science & Monitoring

Support field research and biodiversity monitoring that guide long-term strategies for species

recovery and habitat management.

Together, we are building a future where birds and people thrive in harmony.
